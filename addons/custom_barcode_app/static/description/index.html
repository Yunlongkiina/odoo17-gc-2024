Barcode App

Inside your warehouse it is hard to enter items manually to create a transfer or to validate a purchase order delivery order etc. That is when the barcode scanning is come into play. If you can do all the work with barcodes it is much more easier and error free. So this is the ultimate solution.You can use this barcode app to create transfers, Validate warehouse operations, Inventory adjustments, View Product details.Refer to the tutorial for more details

Warehouse Operations

When you receive purchase order or prepare a delivery order you need to validate the products quantity you send. This time warehouse operation function is a life savor. This helps to reduce inventory errors. This will help your business to deliver correct products to your customer.

Add products and change Counted quantity by barcode scanner

Use the barcode scanner to change the counted quantity of the product or scan a new product to add them to the warehouse operation. If user add new product to the operation it will shown in red line because the that product was unexpected in the operation.

Handle multiple Locations

Smart Button Panel

To change the product quantity manually this module gives you smart button panel. This panel allows you to

  2. Add one to the product quantity
  3. Remove one to the product quantity
  4. Equalize the demanded quantity and scanned quantity
